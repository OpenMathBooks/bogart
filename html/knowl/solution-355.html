Our recurrence becomes \(a_n=a_{n-1}+a_{n-2}\text{,}\) and following the pattern of ProblemÂ 213 we get

\begin{align*} \sum_{n=2}^\infty a_nx^n \amp= \sum_{n=2}^\infty a_{n-1}x^n + \sum_{n=2}^\infty a_{n-2}x^n\\ \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n-a_0-a_1x \amp= x\left(\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_{n}x^{n}-a_0\right) + x^2\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_{n}x^n\\ (1-x-x^2)\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n \amp= a_0+a_1x-a_0x\\ \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n \amp= \frac{a_0+a_1x-a_0x}{(1-x-x^2)}\text{.} \end{align*}

Since now \(a_0=a_1=1\text{,}\) we have \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n= \frac{1}{1-x-x^2}\text{.}\)
