\(\left(A-\frac{1200m}{p}\right)\left(1+\frac{.01p}{12}\right)^n+\frac{1200m}{p}=0\) gives us the equation \(\left(1+\frac{.01p}{12}\right)^n=\frac{1200m}{1200m-Ap}\text{.}\) Taking logarithms to any base we choose gives us \(n\log (1+\frac{.01p}{12})=\log 1200m-\log (1200m-Ap)\) and so \(n=\frac{\log 1200m-\log (1200m-Ap)}{\log (1+\frac{.01p}{12})}\text{.}\)
