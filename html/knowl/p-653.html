There are two operations on graphs that we can apply to get a recurrence (though a more general kind than those we have studied for sequences) which will let us compute the number of spanning trees of a graph. The operations each apply to an edge \(e\) of a graph \(G\text{.}\) The first is called deletion; we delete the edge \(e\) from the graph by removing it from the edge set.
