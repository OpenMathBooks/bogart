\(a_n=a_{n-1} +2\text{.}\) Then by substitution \(a_n=a_{n-2}+2+2\text{,}\) and so we conjecture that \(a_n = 20 +2n\text{.}\) Since she adds two dollars to her savings each week for \(n\) weeks, she has adds 2n dollars to her original 20, which proves the formula. We could have used induction to prove it as well.
