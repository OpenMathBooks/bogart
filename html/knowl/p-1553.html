The group of all rotations of the square is simply the set of the four powers of the cycle \(\rho = (1\ 2\ 3\ 4)\text{.}\) for this reason it is called a cyclic group 3  and is often denoted by \(C_4\text{.}\) Similarly, the rotation group of an \(n\)-gon is usually denoted \(C_n\text{.}\)
