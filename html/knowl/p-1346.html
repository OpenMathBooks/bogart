Let \(T\) be the set of all \(i\) such that \(x\in A_i\text{.}\) Then \(x \in A_i\) for exactly those \(i \in T\text{.}\) Note that \(|T| \gt 0\) because \(x\) is in at least one \(A_i\text{.}\) Then the coefficient of \(P(x)\) is \(\sum_{S:S\subseteq[n],S\ne\emptyset} (-1)^{|S|-1}\text{.}\) But

\begin{equation*} \sum_{S:S\subseteq[n],S\ne\emptyset} (-1)^{|S|-1} = -\sum_{i=1}^T\binom{|T|}{i}(-1)^i = -[(1-1)^{|T|} - 1] = 0+1 = 1. \end{equation*}

Thus if \(x \in E_P(\bigcup_{i=1}^n A_i)\text{,}\) then the coefficient of \(P(x)\) in \(E_P(\bigcup_{i=1}^n A_i)\) is 1.
