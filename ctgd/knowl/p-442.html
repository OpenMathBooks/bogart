Now suppose that when \(n=k-1\text{,}\) \((x+y)^n=\sum_{i=0}^n \binom{n}{i}x^{n-i}y^i.\) This gives us

\begin{align*} (x+y)^k =\amp (x+y)(x+y)^{k-1}=(x+y)\sum_{i=0}^{k-1}\binom{k-1}{i}x^{k-1-i}y^i\\ =\amp \sum_{i=0}^{k-1}\binom{k-1}{i}x^{k-i}y^i+\sum_{i=0}^{k-1} \binom{k-1}{i}x^{k-1-i}y^{i+1}\\ =\amp \sum_{i=0}^{k-1}\binom{k-1}{i}x^{k-i}y^i+\sum_{i=1}^{k} \binom{k-1}{i-1}x^{k-i}y^{i}\\ =\amp x^k+\left(\sum_{i=1}^{k-1}\binom{k-1}{i}x^{k-i}y^i+\binom{k-1}{i-1}x^{k-i}y^i\right) +y^k\\ =\amp x^k+\left(\sum_{i=1}^{k-1} \binom{k}{i}x^{k-i}y^i\right) +y^k\ =\ \sum_{i=0}^k \binom{k}{i}x^{k-i}y^i. \end{align*} in-context
