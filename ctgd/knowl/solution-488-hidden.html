The 180 degree rotation sends the diagonals to themselves, i.e., it fixes the diagonals. \(\varphi_{1|3}\) fixes the diagonals. So does \(\varphi_{2|4}\) and the identity. However, \(\rho\text{,}\) \(\rho^3\text{,}\) \(\varphi_{12|34}\) and \(\varphi_{14|23}\) all exchange the diagonals. Thus four elements fix each diagonal and four elements interchange them.
