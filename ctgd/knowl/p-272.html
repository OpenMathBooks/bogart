Problem 47 has a geometric interpretation in a coordinate plane. A lattice path in the plane is a “curve” made up of line segments that either go from a point \((i,j)\) to the point \((i+1,j)\) or from a point \((i,j)\) to the point \((i,j+1)\text{,}\) where \(i\) and \(j\) are integers. (Thus lattice paths always move either up or to the right.) The length of the path is the number of such line segments.
