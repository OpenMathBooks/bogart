Other examples of recurrences are

\begin{equation} a_n = a_{n-1} + 7,\tag{2.3} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} a_n =3a_{n-1} + 2^n,\tag{2.4} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} a_n = a_{n-1} + 3a_{n-2},\mbox{ and}\tag{2.5} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} a_n= a_1a_{n-1} + a_2a_{n-2}+\cdots + a_{n-1}a_1.\tag{2.6} \end{equation}
