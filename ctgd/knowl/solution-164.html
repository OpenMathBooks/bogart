Solution 94.1

\(a_n =a_o+cn\text{.}\) The formula is valid with \(n=0\text{,}\) and if \(a_{n-1}=a_0 +c(n-1)\text{,}\) then \(a_n = a_0 +c(n-1) +c =a_0+cn\text{.}\) Therefore the fact that \(a_{n-1}=a_0+ca_{n-1}\) implies the fact that \(a_n=a_0+cn\text{.}\) Therefore by the principle of mathematical induction, \(a_n=a_0+cn\) for all nonnegative integers \(n\text{.}\)
