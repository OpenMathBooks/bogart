To show that \(\overline{\sigma}\circ\overline{\tau} = \overline{\sigma\circ\tau}\text{,}\) we note that

\begin{align*} (\overline{\sigma}\circ\overline{\tau})(\{i,j\}) =\amp \overline{\sigma}(\overline{\tau}(\{i,j\})=\overline{\sigma}(\{\tau(i). \tau(j)\})=\\ \sigma({\tau(i),\tau(j)}) =\amp \{(\sigma\circ\tau)(i),(\sigma\circ\tau)(j)\} =\\ \amp \overline{\sigma\circ\tau}(\{i,j\})\text{.} \end{align*}
