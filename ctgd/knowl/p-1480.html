We do the same in the case that \(m-n\) is negative, getting

\begin{align*} \varphi^m\circ(\varphi^{-(n-1)}\circ\varphi^{-1}) =\amp \varphi^{m-(n-1)}\circ\varphi^{-1}\\ \varphi^m\circ(\varphi^{-1})^{n-1}\varphi^{-1} =\amp (\varphi^{-1})^{n-m-1}\circ\varphi^{-1}\\ \varphi^m\circ(\varphi^{-1})^n =\amp (\varphi^{-1})^{n-m}\\ \varphi^m\circ\varphi^{-n} =\amp \varphi^{m-n}. \end{align*}
