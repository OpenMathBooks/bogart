\begin{align*} \sum_{n=2}^\infty c_n\frac{x^{n-1}}{(n-1)!} \! =\amp \!\sum_{n=2}^\infty(n-1) c_{n-2}\frac{x^{n-1}}{(n-1)!} + \sum_{n=2}^\infty c_{n-1}\frac{x^{n-1}}{(n-1)!}\\ \sum_{n=1}^\infty c_n\frac{x^{n-1}}{(n-1)!}- c_1 \! =\amp \! x\sum_{n=2}^\infty c_{n-2}\frac{x^{n-2}}{(n-2)!} + \sum_{n=0}^\infty c_n\frac{x^n}{n!} -c_0\\ T'(x)\! =\amp \!xT(x) +T(x) \end{align*}

\(T(0) = c_0 =1\text{.}\) Then \(\frac{T'(x)}{T(x)} = x+1\text{,}\) giving \(\ln T(x) =x^2/2+x+k\text{,}\) and \(T(x) =e^ke^{x+ x^2/2}=e^{x+x^2/2}\text{,}\) since \(T(0)=1\text{.}\)
