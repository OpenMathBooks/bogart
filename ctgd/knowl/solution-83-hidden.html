For each ten dollar bill take a rightstep and for each twenty dollar bill take an upstep (where rightstep and upstep have the hopefully natural meaning). The assumption that there are an equal number of ten and twenty dollar bills means that the path will end up at \((n,n)\text{.}\) Each sequence of tens and twenties gives a lattice path and each lattice path corresponds to such a sequence, so we have a bijection.
