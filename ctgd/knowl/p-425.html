You can also base a proof on the observation that \((x+y+x)^n= \sum_{k=0}^n\binom{n}{k}(x+y)^kz^{n-k}\) and \((x+y+z)^n=\sum_{m=0}^n\binom{n}{m}x^m(y+z)^{n-m}\) and asking for the coefficient of \(x^my^{n-m-k}z^k\text{.}\) You do have to use the binomial theorem with an eye to the result you are looking for, however.
