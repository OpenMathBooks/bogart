By the product principle for EGFs, the EGF for the right hand side is

\begin{equation*} e^x\cdot xe^x =xe^{2x}=x\sum_{i=o}^\infty \frac{(2x)^i}{i!} = \sum_{j=1}^\infty 2^{j-1}\frac{x^j}{(j-1)!}=\sum_{j=1}^\infty j2^{j-1}\frac{x^j}{j!}. \end{equation*}

Thus the coefficient of \(\frac{x^n}{n!}\) is \(n2^{n-1}\text{,}\) as well as \(\sum_{k=1}^n \binom{n}{k}k\text{.}\)
