Adding \(\frac{1}{k(k+1)}\) to both sides of this equation gives us

\begin{align*} \sum_{i=1}^{k-1} \frac{1}{i(i+1)}+\frac{1}{k(k+1)} =\amp \frac{k-1}{k}+\frac{1}{k(k+1)}\\ \sum_{i=1}^k \frac{1}{i(i+1)} =\amp \frac{(k-1)(k+1)}{k(k+1)}+\frac{1}{k(k+1)}\\ =\amp \frac{k^2 -1 +1}{k(k+1)}\\ =\amp \frac{k}{k+1}. \end{align*}
