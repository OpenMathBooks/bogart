It is a permutation because the composition of permutations is a permutation. (You could be more precise and use the inductive definition of \(\varphi^n\) to prove inductively that \(\varphi^n\) is a permutation.) We expect \(\varphi^m\circ\varphi^n=\varphi^{m+n}\text{,}\) and we expect \((\varphi^m)n=\varphi^{mn}\text{,}\) and we would prove this by induction.
